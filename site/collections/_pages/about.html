Hello there! Do you share my passion for building communities and making a positive impact on them?

Let me tell you a little about myself: in 2015, I started a business from scratch and successfully scaled it within a year. In 2017, I handed it off to my partners and transitioned to working as a graphic designer. After four years, I discovered the world of community building.

However, even before then, I had been building communities without knowing the formal terminology for it. For example, I created successful gaming communities on Discord and effectively expanded them over time, eventually delegating responsibilities to other passionate users.

I aim to bring together like-minded individuals with the goal of creating a strong sense of community and significant business value.

Nothing motivates me more than helping others and bringing people together.

Lastly, I am a huge animal lover, with a soft spot for creatures of all shapes and sizes, whether they live on land, in the mountains, or in the sea and sky.

And I am always just a ping away!

According to me,

Community Builders are the Unsung Superheroes who don’t wear capes.​​​​​​
